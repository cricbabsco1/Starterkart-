FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

How fast can my website be delivered?
+
Delivery ranges from 3 days (Premium Plan, priority) to 5 days (Growth Plan). Basic Plan websites are delivered within 4 days from the day we receive your brand details and product photos.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
+
Yes — every Starterkart website is built mobile-first. Over 90% of fashion buyers in India shop on mobile, so we design for that experience before anything else.
Do you upload my products for me?
+
Yes. Product upload is included in every plan — from 20–40 products (Basic) up to 70–120 products (Premium). Just send us your photos and details.
Can I request changes after launch?
+
Absolutely. Basic includes 10 requested changes, Premium includes 25. The Premium Plan also comes with 3 months of ongoing support.
Do you work with Instagram-only brands?
+
Yes — many of our clients are Instagram-first brands looking to upgrade to a professional website. We specialize in helping IG businesses look more trustworthy and convert better.
What if I need something custom?
+
Custom quotations are available for advanced requirements. Just reach out via WhatsApp or email and we'll prepare a tailored proposal.
